About Sania:

Sania Blu is a senior studying Sports Broadcast at the University of Missouri-Columbia. She is a California native but currently lives in Chicago, IL. Sania has experience in a variety of multimedia forums. In addition to her work as journalist, Sania is also in the Honors College, a mentor, and she participates in several organizations on campus. Sania is also the recipient of several academic awards including the University of Missouri Honors College Hayden Scholarship (2018) and Hesburgh Award (2017).

Sania's passion for journalism comes from her interest in storytelling. She is a firm believer in the idea that everyone has a story in which there are so many pages and words that led to this very moment in someone's live.

In her spare time, Sania performs with her competitive dance team and tries to travel as much as she can. Sania also values spending time with her family and friends to create lasting memories.
